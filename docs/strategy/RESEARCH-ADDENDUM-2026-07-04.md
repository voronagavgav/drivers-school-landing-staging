The market leader itself signals one-time as the preferred model. This kills any residual case for leading with option B (monthly sub).

5. **Reference frame vs. alternatives:** автошкола theory courses run ~2,999–7,000 грн; paid НМТ prep runs 999–2,800 грн/subject (~284–407 грн/lesson) [https://buki.com.ua/news/pidgotovka-do-nmt-skilki-dovedetsia-zaplatiti-shhob-otrimati-visokii-bal/]. A ~399 грн intelligence layer is 6–20× cheaper than the formal path.
6. **Free-anchor risk (new, flagged):** the closest "pay-to-pass-an-exam" analogs — iLearn and EdEra for ЗНО/НМТ — are entirely free [https://ilearn.org.ua/]. Different payer (parents) and product shape, but it strengthens the requirement that the paid layer be legibly DIFFERENT from "access to tests" (which is free everywhere, including the state's 900k-user simulator).
7. **Positioning white space (new):** no competitor anywhere in the market uses a «доступ до іспиту» / until-you-pass frame — the dominant one-time label is «Назавжди/Forever» (Vodiy). Exam-scoped framing is genuinely unclaimed and aligns exactly with the P(pass)-dial narrative.

This does not move the recommendation: 399 sits correctly relative to both anchors (a legible premium over the 380 web anchor, comfortably under the ≈500 IAP ceiling).

**Recommendation absorbed:** strategy §8 option C reprices from 499 → **399 грн one-time**, with the fail-safe completion offer unchanged. 499 remains a defensible price-test arm, not the default. A live pricing-page smoke test (§2) remains the final confirmation step, but the band question is closed for build purposes.
