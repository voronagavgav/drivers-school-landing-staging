## 2. LEGAL VERDICT ⚠️ *(OPINION — no legal research track ran; this is not legal advice; get a Ukrainian IP lawyer before launch)*

**(a) Official question text — risk: LOW-to-MODERATE, likely tolerable.**
- Mitigating facts (evidenced): the state publishes the bank freely and openly (https://pdr.infotech.gov.ua/instructions/160), and multiple commercial incumbents reproduce the full question set verbatim, including at per-question URL granularity (https://pdrtest.com/question/16.2.13), apparently without visible enforcement. Widespread, long-standing market practice by named companies is not a legal defense, but it is a real signal about enforcement posture.
- Residual uncertainty (opinion): Ukrainian law exempts certain official/state documents from copyright, but whether the exam question bank qualifies is exactly the kind of question that needs counsel. Also verify no terms-of-use on the gov platform restrict reproduction.
- Required mitigation regardless: never imply official status (already a product constraint); attribute the source of the question bank plainly ("на основі офіційних питань ГСЦ МВС" phrasing needs a lawyer's sign-off too — "офіційних" is a loaded word).

**(b) Restyled official images — risk: MODERATE, our biggest legal exposure, but also the most mitigatable.**
- Opinion: restyling is creating derivative works of the official illustrations. That's a *worse* posture than verbatim reproduction if the images are protected, and a *better* one if the underlying works are unprotected state material — same open question as (a).
- Mitigations available: (i) our images are transformative in style while the *informational content* (road situations, signs — which are themselves standardized by ПДР regulation, arguably uncopyrightable as normative content) is what's retained; (ii) we can, if ever challenged, re-generate scenes from scratch to spec rather than edit-from-official — the restyle pipeline already proves we can produce the style; keep that option documented as the fallback; (iii) road signs themselves are defined by government regulation and drawing our own renditions of standardized signs is common practice across every competitor's "sign catalog."
- **Verdict: proceed, budget one legal consult (this is a bounded, one-time question), and keep the "re-generate from scratch" fallback alive.** Do not let this block launch prep.

## 3. STATE-COMPETITOR THREAT

**The threat is real and permanent.** pdr.infotech.gov.ua is not a neglected gov page — it's a scaled product (900,000+ students, 4.8 self-reported rating, users reportedly spending ~47 min/day) that ranks at/near the top of most commercial queries. *(EVIDENCE: https://pdr.infotech.gov.ua/ — keywords track.)* It owns "офіційні тести" intent outright. Cede those terms.

**What survives it (the moat):**
- **Honest readiness signal.** No competitor — state or private — offers P(pass); the market's best is "pass every ticket twice" folk heuristics. *(EVIDENCE — keywords track found this uncontested.)* The state site tells you if you answered correctly; it does not tell you if you're *ready*. Given ~80% first-attempt failure *despite* 900k people using the free official tool, "practicing on the official site" demonstrably ≠ passing — that gap IS our product argument.
- **Restyled images + calm premium UX** — the state tool competes on authority, not on experience or on anxiety reduction.
- **Finite "план до іспиту"** — the state offers an infinite question pile, not a plan. *(OPINION on salience — jtbd track returned nothing, see §4.)*

Implicit marketing line (opinion, legally clean): "900 тисяч людей тренуються безкоштовно — і 4 з 5 все одно провалюють. Питання не в доступі до тестів."

## 4. LEARNER REALITY ⚠️ *(the jtbd track returned NO evidence — this section is inference from adjacent verified facts + opinion; re-run the track before writing onboarding copy)*

What we *can* infer from verified exam mechanics *(EVIDENCE — failstats)*:
- **Retake loop = 10-day mandatory wait + 250 грн per attempt + unlimited tries + theory valid only 1 year** (expires if practical isn't passed in time). So the learner's real costs are *repeated fees, calendar time, and the validity clock* — not "limited chances."
- **Implication — retention matters, but in a specific shape:** the natural engagement arc is a *finite sprint to a date* (first attempt), plus a *re-activation moment* (the 10-day retake window after a fail — a precisely timed, high-anxiety re-engagement trigger no competitor exploits). Build for sprint completion + retake win-back, not for indefinite habit.
- **Implication — "план до іспиту" is plausibly the hero,** because it matches both the finite-sprint reality and the anxiety gap (§6). But calling it *the* hero is opinion until jtbd data exists.
- **Top-failed topics are known** (MVS, Dec 2025): traffic-controller gestures, unregulated intersections, roundabouts, road markings, first aid — front-load these in the plan and in content marketing. *(EVIDENCE: https://tsn.ua/auto/news/avto_mir/provaliuiut-teoretychnyy-ispyt-masovo-u-mvs-nazvaly-5-pomylok-iaki-dopuskaiut-maybutni-vodiyi-2981843.html)*

## 6. ACQUISITION

**Keyword gaps worth owning** *(EVIDENCE — keywords track, with volumes unmeasured/inferred — no paid tools were accessible)*:
1. **Exam anxiety** — «боюся не скласти іспит пдр» etc.: 8/10 results are generic ЗНО/НМТ school psychology; **no ПДР-prep platform ranks at all**. Not literally zero ПДР content (a Village guide + one autoschool blog appear), but the gap is real and matches our positioning exactly. Own it with a calm, expert anxiety-content hub.
2. **Programmatic long-tail: 2,322 per-question pages + topic pages.** Proven format — pdrtest.com ranks individual /question/ URLs. Our restyled images make these pages *visibly* better than incumbents'.
3. **«Найскладніші питання ПДР»** — demand served weakly (gov's "100 hardest", manualov.net, scattered YouTube/TikTok). Winnable, and pairs with the top-5-failed-topics data.
4. **Superior sign encyclopedia** — informational SERPs are beatable and the restyled-image differentiator is strongest here.
5. **Viral intersection puzzles** («хто проїде першим») — owned by news portals, ignored by prep brands; use for links/awareness with our visuals.
- Table stakes: stamp the year token («2026») and trust signaling like every incumbent — while never claiming official status.
- **Cede:** «тести пдр 2026», «офіційні тести» head terms (gov + vodiy.ua own them).

**Автошкола channel verdict: worth one cheap structured experiment, not a launch bet.** *(EVIDENCE for facts, OPINION for verdict.)* Channel is ~1,753 schools, geographically concentrated (start Kyiv/Dnipro/Lviv/Odesa). German precedent (Fahren Lernen, school-distributed serials, "10M успішних") proves the B2B2C shape — but its moat is a *proprietary licensed question bank*, which doesn't exist in UA (questions are free). Also: schools already monetize their own ~1,000 грн online-theory tier, so a paid per-seat pitch reads as cannibalization. Viable framings only: **free value-add** (school-branded, improves their pass stats → their marketing) or **white-label/revenue-share**. No UA template exists — greenfield first-mover upside, unproven channel. Test with 5-10 schools in one oblast before building any white-label machinery.
