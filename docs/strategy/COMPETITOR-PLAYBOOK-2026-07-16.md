## Pricing plays

- SHOW THE PRICE ON THE LANDING, PLAINLY. Zutobi never shows a price (dark-pattern trial-to-sub funnel); pddtest.kz hides «/мес» in fine print. We do the inverse as a trust weapon: «399 ₴ · один раз» in large type on the pricing card, zero asterisks, zero fine print. The transparency itself is a category differentiator — say so implicitly by adjacency to the trust band.
- ONE offer, ONE card. pdds.kz ships 4 tiers identical except duration (pure price discrimination, decision friction); driving-tests sells 3 windows of the same features. We ship a single card: 399 ₴ «доступ до іспиту». Build the number from a constants module so the 499 ₴ L3 smoke arm can swap without touching copy.
- NEGATE THE CATEGORY'S BILLING SINS EXPLICITLY. Every studied paid competitor uses auto-renew subs, trial-that-converts, or time-boxed rental. Put the negation right on the card as three short lines: «Не підписка. Без автосписань. Не згорає за 30 днів.» (respect the copy-gate: «підписк» only in negated form on the same line). This out-frames Vodiy's «Назавжди», GreenWay's sub, ManGo's calendar window in one stroke — the brief's verified-unclaimed framing, now with competitor-proven pain behind each line.
- LOSS-AVOIDANCE MATH, HONEST VERSION. driving-tests' best price frame is «Premium costs less than a rebooking ($25–50 fee + 2–4 weeks waiting)». Ours: «Дешевше за одну провалену спробу» + the concrete mechanics (кожна спроба — 250 ₴; 4 з 5 провалюють першу). Optionally the автошкола anchor (теорія в автошколі 2 999–7 000 ₴) — real reference points only, NEVER a strikethrough price, NEVER invented savings math (driving-tests' «save $26 vs 3 monthly subs» is fabricated-reference anchoring — banned). Do not print Vodiy's 380 ₴ without a fresh check (doNot).
- TWO-COLUMN FREE/PAID BOUNDARY, WITH FREE LISTED FIRST AND LONGER. Vodiy labels free vs Premium clearly but gates explanations (the #1 resentment). Our split is the clean counter-story: LEFT «Безкоштовно назавжди»: усі 2322 офіційні питання · пояснення до кожного · зображення · симулятор іспиту 20/20/2. RIGHT «399 ₴ — доступ до іспиту»: деталізація дилу готовності · FSRS-план до твоєї дати · нагадування · аналітика помилок. The paid column must read as intelligence, never as unlocking content.
- FAIL-SAFE OFFER IN THE GUARANTEE SLOT. driving-tests proves a de-risk promise on the price card converts an anxious buyer; pddtest.kz shows the anti-pattern (a «Гарантия сдачи» slogan with zero terms). We take the POSITION, not the wording: the completion-tied offer («пройшов весь план, але не склав → доступ безкоштовно до наступної спроби», exact wording pends L1) sits directly on/under the price card with its condition stated in one honest sentence. Never the word «гарантія» un-negated; never pass-tied.
- TRUST BAND TOUCHING THE PRICE. Vodiy's honesty FAQ («It wouldn't be honest to guarantee a pass») is its most trust-building line — ours goes adjacent to the number, not buried: «прогрес не зникає · без автосписань · одна ціна». Category complaints (lost progress, billing confusion, broken restores) map 1:1 to these three chips.
- NAME WHAT THE PRICE IS ANCHORED TO. «Доступ до іспиту» needs one clarifying line so it doesn't read as a calendar window: «прив'язано до твого іспиту, а не до календаря». The exam-date picker section upstream sets this up; the pricing card pays it off.

## Acquisition plays

- ZERO-FRICTION VALUE BEFORE ANY ASK — the whole category's strongest funnel move (Vodiy's no-signup random card, pdds' unregistered 40-question trial, driving-tests' «no registration required»). Our hero one-question mechanic already beats all of them (instant, in-viewport, no network); keep «Спробувати без реєстрації» as the visible secondary path and never interrupt free flow with a pitch (driving-tests' Q10 paywall interstitial is the anti-pattern).
- CONCRETE-NUMBER PROOF CHIPS IN VIEWPORT ONE. Vodiy's floating stat chips (20/20 passed · 117 cards · 65 topics · MIA database) and Zutobi's number strip both work because numbers are specific. Ours: «2322 офіційні питання» (verify exact count first — vodiy says 2321) · «65 тем» · «20 питань · 20 хв · 2 помилки». Only honest, verifiable numbers; restyle to taste (quiet monochrome chips, not multicolor glass).
- AUTHORITY CLAIM STATED ONCE, EARLY, LEGALLY. Every strong competitor leads with official-base provenance (Vodiy «official HSC MIA database», pddtest «одобрены в СпецЦОНах»). Ours is the proof-badge cluster + БАЗА section — «на основі офіційних питань» phrasing ONLY after L1 sign-off, never implying ГСЦ МВС affiliation, with the learning-tool disclaimer in the footer.
- FRESHNESS AS A TRUST SIGNAL. driving-tests year-stamps everything («2026 updated» badge, year in titles); GOV.UK refreshes its fail stat monthly («48 of 100 failed in June 2026»). Adopt: «оновлено 2026» badge (honest under Order №225), the «1 з 5» hook cited «за даними 2026 року», and a visible «база актуальна» chip in the БАЗА section.
- FAQ AS OBJECTION-HANDLING + RICH SNIPPETS. Vodiy's 10–11 honest Q&A with FAQPage JSON-LD is the single best production-completeness steal. Ours (already in brief): чому не підписка · що безкоштовно · чи це офіційний іспит (ні — disclaimer) · як рахується готовність · чи зникне прогрес. Mark up every real question in schema.
- GOOGLE ONE-TAP AS THE ONLY SIGN-IN FRICTION-REDUCER (Vodiy hero pattern) — one-tap secondary next to the primary CTA path, never an auth-gate modal intercepting progress-save (Vodiy's own dark spot).
- RETAKER RE-ACTIVATION LINE. GOV.UK is the only site addressing a second audience (supervisors) with its own track; nobody addresses the just-failed retaker — the highest-anxiety, precisely-timed segment. One line on the landing («Не склав? …») + later a dedicated intent page; soft-check the «10 днів» figure before it ships.
- REAL COUNTERS ONLY, WHEN REAL. driving-tests' ticking «5,334 tests today» converts but is scarcity theater. Our version: honest cumulative counters (відповідей дано / іспитів симульовано) added to the БАЗА section only once real data exists — cold-start rule: zero fabricated counts, zero fake-live tickers.
- RESERVE THE CALIBRATION-REPORT SLOT. Zutobi's 95.8% and driving-tests' 97% are the category's best converters AND its biggest lies (unsourced). Our future «Коли дил показує 90+, складають X%» is the honest killer — design the pattern/slot in the БАЗА section now, ship NOTHING until PassOutcome data exists.
- LATER (project-level, not landing scope): programmatic UA long-tail — per-topic pages (знаки, розмітка, жести регулювальника…, 65 topics = 65 URLs), per-category (A/B/C), intent pages («тести ПДР 2026 безкоштовно», «не склав іспит ПДР що далі» — the retaker SERP vacuum), reference guides à la Vodiy /rozmitka/. Vodiy + driving-tests prove this is the category's #1 channel. Also decide a conscious robots policy (Vodiy blocks AI crawlers + SEO scrapers; pddtest's Clean-param dedupes referral URLs — copy that for campaign links).

## Design patterns (taste-filtered)

- PRODUCT-IN-ACTION HERO (Zutobi's real question card with the green-highlighted answer is the category's best hero visual; taste demands 'show the real product once, early, beautifully'): our hero one-tap official question IS this pattern, upgraded from screenshot to live interaction. Render it as a single rounded (12–24px) softly-shadowed card cropped by the fold to pull scroll — no fake browser chrome, no device mock.
- DIAL SECTION = SOLIDROAD PANEL PATTERN: a floating light panel with real metrics glowing against an atmospheric ground (their 951/24/80% panel on dusk photography). The live decaying dial sits in exactly that treatment — one real number, generous air, caption «Це не % пройденого» as a muted one-liner. The dial is our single signature art element (the Stripe-ribbon slot); nothing else on the page gets a competing atmospheric or gradient.
- QUIET STAT CHIPS, NOT GLASS CONFETTI: Vodiy's floating multicolor glass chips prove hero-adjacent stats work; taste's one-accent rule strips them to 2–3 monochrome/hairline-bordered chips with one accent maximum. Same information density, editorial restraint.
- ONE CTA STYLE, REPEATED: pdds' single full-width pill and Zutobi's 4×-repeated orange pill both prove consistency converts. Taste version: 44–56px near-black (or single-accent) pill «Почати безкоштовно», one ghost secondary «Спробувати без реєстрації», identical styling at every decision depth; on mobile, full-width stacked pills (our verified bright spot).
- FULL-BLEED STAT INTERSTITIAL: GOV.UK's tinted stat band («48 of every 100 failed…») + taste's color-block chapter break merge into one pattern — a deep-toned full-bleed band carrying ONE big statement («Кожна спроба — 250 ₴» / loss frame) and one CTA, breaking the light rhythm before pricing.
- NUMBERED MECHANISM ROW: driving-tests' «What makes us different 01–03» maps onto taste's monospace-eyebrow/Greptile register — a 3-step thin-line-icon row explaining the mechanism plainly (відповідаєш → план повторень → дил калібрується). Counters the category's unexplained-«smart» anti-pattern with transparency.
- SECTION RHYTHM ENGINE (steal #2 + Vodiy's whole-page finish): every section = bold header + muted ONE-line sub-label + consistent grid; 120–200px vertical padding; hairline rules and dotted dividers instead of bordered cards; max 3–4 items per feature row. The market leader wins on whole-page polish — we must not be a great hero above an empty page (pdds' dead-void body is the cautionary example).
- FAQ AS HAIRLINE ACCORDION: Vodiy's accordion, restyled — hairline dividers, no boxed cards, generous line-height.
- FOOTER = BRAND MOMENT + PRODUCTION COMPLETENESS: taste's dark ground with oversized ghosted wordmark, carrying Vodiy's comprehensive link columns + the legal disclaimer. Both, merged.
- THEME-AWARE: every one of the 7 sites is light-only. Ship dark mode for a phone-first evening-study audience — cheap differentiation the whole category misses.
- REJECTED BY TASTE FILTER (even where competitors prove them): Zutobi's zig-zag text↔media rows (taste: template SaaS sameness — use headline-left/paragraph-right two-column instead); Vodiy's multicolor icon tiles and lifestyle stock photo hero; pddtest's blue+yellow all-caps shouting + emoji headings; pdds' generic cyan→magenta gradient hero; driving-tests' selfie collage hero (no real testimonials exist for us anyway — cold-start); screenshot-testimonial mosaics; carousels; any live ticking counters; cookie/consent UI overlapping the fold (Zutobi's modal covers its own feature strip).

## SEO checklist (landing route)

- TITLE pattern (Next.js Metadata API, one constants module): year-stamped + keyword-first + benefit tail, ≤60 chars — e.g. «Тести ПДР 2026 онлайн — тренер готовності до іспиту». Lessons: Vodiy leads with «official base», driving-tests year-stamps («FREE … Practice Test 2026»), pdds stamps «2026» for freshness. Target head terms: «тести ПДР», «іспит ПДР 2026», «ПДР онлайн безкоштовно».
- META DESCRIPTION ~150–160 chars, Ukrainian, hand-proofread (GOV.UK shipped «Get ready to your driving test» — even state teams don't proofread): official base + безкоштовно + the readiness question, e.g. «Усі офіційні питання ПДР 2026 безкоштовно: пояснення, зображення, симулятор іспиту. Дізнайся, чи ти справді готовий скласти з першої спроби.»
- EXACTLY ONE REAL <h1> in server-rendered HTML = the hero headline slot text (identical across A/B variants' structure). Vodiy has NO literal h1 (a paragraph fills the slot); pdds serves an empty SPA shell to crawlers. Next.js App Router SSR gives us this free — verify with view-source that headline + section copy exist without JS.
- H2s = section headers phrased in search vocabulary: «Симулятор іспиту ПДР — 20 питань · 20 хвилин · 2 помилки», «План підготовки до іспиту», «Питання і відповіді» — each doubles as a long-tail match.
- JSON-LD @graph (Vodiy's is the category benchmark — match and exceed): Organization, WebSite, WebApplication (applicationCategory EducationalApplication, isAccessibleForFree: true, plus one Offer: 399 UAH, priceCurrency UAH, one-time), FAQPage built from the REAL accordion Q&A (10+ items → rich snippets), later BreadcrumbList on subpages. Zutobi/pdds/pddtest all ship zero-or-thin schema — this is an easy win.
- LOCALE HYGIENE: html lang="uk", og:locale "uk_UA" (GOV.UK ships en-US on a UK site; Vodiy leaves Ukrainian OG titles on /en pages). Self-canonical on the landing route; add hreflang ONLY when real locale routes exist — never a Zutobi-style canonical pointing at /default.
- OG/TWITTER: dedicated 1200×630 share image (not a logo — pdds' og:image is a bare logo), og:title/description mirroring the meta, twitter:card summary_large_image.
- ROBOTS + SITEMAP: sitemap.xml from day one with real lastmod (pddtest's 2024-stale sitemap on a «2026» brand is the anti-pattern); allow search crawlers; make a CONSCIOUS AI-crawler decision (Vodiy blocks GPTBot/ClaudeBot/Ahrefs deliberately); add Clean-param/UTM hygiene for campaign links (pddtest's one good trick).
- NUMBERS AS CONSTANTS: bank count (2322 vs vodiy's 2321 — run the count check before printing), 21.5% first-attempt stat, 399 price — all in one module feeding copy, title, meta, and schema atomically, so a bank revision is a one-line bump.
- LATER (noted, not landing): per-topic/per-intent programmatic pages with unique titles («Знаки дорожнього руху — тести ПДР 2026»), the retaker intent page («не склав іспит ПДР»), and Person/author E-E-A-T markup once content pages exist (driving-tests' 3,720-URL sitemap is the ceiling to study).

## Anti-patterns (never do)

- Fake urgency/scarcity: countdown timers on evergreen pages (pddtest's 03:00:00), «bought in past month» tickers, live «practicing now» counters (driving-tests) — hard-gated by scripts/funnel-donot-guard.sh; never even in code comments on funnel surfaces.
- Phantom pricing: permanent strikethrough anchors (pdds 30,000→10,800 «-60%», pddtest 84,990→70,000), stacked discount badges, fabricated «you save X» math against reference prices nobody pays (driving-tests' $26).
- Hidden or disguised billing: price absent from the landing (Zutobi), «/мес» in fine print while the card reads one-time (pddtest), subscriptions dressed as «7-day access windows» sold to a one-time-need audience (driving-tests), 3-day trials that auto-charge with discretionary refunds (Zutobi).
- Renting the content: time-boxed access to the question bank (pdds 30/60/90/365), gating explanations behind Premium (Vodiy — the market's #1 resentment), mid-free-test paywall interrupts (driving-tests' Q10 checkpoint), auth-gate modals intercepting progress-save (Vodiy). Our content is free end-to-end, full stop.
- Unverifiable vanity stats: «97% pass rate» (driving-tests), «95.8%» + «America's #1» (Zutobi), «40%→70%» with no methodology (pddtest) — we ship no aggregate outcome claim until real calibration data exists.
- Guarantee slogans without terms: pddtest's «Гарантия сдачи экзамена» with zero conditions/refund mechanism; also any pass-guarantee at all (our doNot — only the completion-tied offer, wording via L1).
- Claiming readiness without measuring it: Vodiy's «your real exam readiness» copy over a product with no readiness signal — the exact credibility gap we exploit; our dial must always be a computed, decaying, calibrated number, never asserted prose or a static checklist (GOV.UK's inert 5-point list).

## Conflicts (competitor practice vs our strategy — strategy wins)

- MONEY-BACK PASS GUARANTEE: driving-tests' repeated «100% money-back if you don't pass» is arguably the category's strongest single conversion lever for anxious buyers, and Zutobi runs a conditioned version. Our doNots ban any pass-tied promise outright. STRATEGY WINS — we adopt only the POSITION (a de-risk statement on the price card) filled by the completion-tied offer. FLAG FOR FOUNDER: worth one question to the L1 lawyer whether a completion-tied money-BACK variant (refund instead of extended access) is legally distinct enough — it tested strongest in-category; no copy changes until L1 rules.
