# Landing head-to-head: OURS vs VODIY

**Ours** — Drivers School · https://voronagavgav.github.io/pdr-landing-preview/ · calm liquid-glass, honest readiness dial, restyled official images, 399 UAH one-time.
**Vodiy** — vodiy.ua · UA market leader (1M+ users) · modern SaaS, photo hero, mode grid, blue/violet accent, 380 UAH one-time.

Judged research-wise against `pdr-design/web-design-craft.md` + conversion best practice. Every verdict was bias-checked; two came back flagged (see §6).

---

## 1) TL;DR

**Ours wins overall — narrowly (3 aspects to 2, one tie).** One-line reason: our landing sells a *differentiated, emotionally-matched outcome* ("go when you're actually ready") and lets an anxious first-timer *experience the product in the hero before committing*, while vodiy is a polished-but-category-generic SaaS page. **But the win is not clean:** vodiy beats us on the two things a young, unknown brand can least afford to lose — **credible social proof at scale** and **whole-page production completeness** — and it edges mobile UX. We win on message and mechanic; vodiy wins on trust and finish. Close the trust gap and it stops being close.

## 3) Where OURS wins (and why it matters)

**Messaging (clear win) — our biggest edge.** The H1 «Складай іспит тоді, коли точно готовий» is an active, outcome-and-feeling promise; vodiy's H1 is a *question* that only names the situation. We name the mechanism («рахує твою готовність за реальними результатами»), reframe price («Дешевше за одну провалену спробу»), and — critically for the anxious learner in the brief — carry explicit permission-to-fail, calm language throughout («Тут можна помилятися — це тренування, а не іспит»). Vodiy's «впевнено, з першої спроби» is performance *pressure*, not reassurance. *Why it matters:* copy that sells the emotional payoff and matches the visitor's state converts better than spec lists (rubric line 17).

**Hero value prop (slight).** Our unique value is legible in the first viewport without scrolling, and we *show the product live* (a real question + image + 4 options as the hero visual) — "what will I actually do here" is answered by demonstration, not claim. Vodiy's real differentiator (1.1M users / 15 years) sits below the fold. We also run a clean single-primary-CTA hierarchy; vodiy splits hero attention between «Почати тест» and a near-equal Google «Увійти».

**Conversion (slight).** The interactive question card means the first action costs zero navigation — product-led "experience value before commitment," the highest-leverage top-of-funnel lever. Plus a commit-vs-try two-CTA pattern («Почати безкоштовно» + «Спробувати без реєстрації», «без картки»), and the primary CTA repeats 5× down the page at each decision depth.

**Visual craft (tie, but our hero is the higher-register surface).** Textbook 60-30-10, a single *reserved* green accent, bespoke restyled official photo + custom SVG signs + the Світлик mascot — vs vodiy's generic stock photo of a man on a phone (which the rubric explicitly penalises).

## 4) Where VODIY wins (the gaps to close)

**Trust / credibility (clear — this is the real gap).** Vodiy shows honest, weighted stat-cards: **15+ років, 1.1M+ користувачів, 256M+ відповідей**, an "Актуально" verification badge on the official base, and App Store / Google Play badges. These are *evidence the product works at scale*. We offer virtue cards (only-official-questions / honest-readiness / no-dark-patterns) — genuinely de-risking and honest, but they are *promises, not proof*. Our only number, «1 з 5 складає з першої спроби», is proof of the *problem*, not of *our* outcomes. An anxious buyer asking "will this actually get me through?" is reassured more by 1.1M people already trusting vodiy than by a young brand's pledge of integrity. Our honesty narrows the gap; it can't close it while the competitor supplies both integrity *and* evidence.

**Production completeness / page rhythm (the reason visual-craft is only a tie).** Vodiy sustains a consistent section template top-to-bottom — bold header + muted sub-label + evenly-padded icon-tile grid — plus a real FAQ accordion, floating glass stat chips bound to the hero, and a comprehensive footer. Our craft is concentrated in the hero; below the fold is unproven in the captures. Whole-page finish is where the market leader looks like a finished product and we look like a great hero.

**Mobile UX (slight).** Vodiy's first viewport is leaner (breadcrumb top bar + one hamburger, short subhead, one in-context photo) vs our dense glass pill (logo + wordmark + CTA + hamburger) plus a 5-6 line paragraph, stat card, two CTAs, trust row and a peeking widget. And vodiy's below-fold vertical mode-launcher (full-width icon+arrow rows, one task per row) gives direct task entry we lack. *Our* mobile bright spot: full-width **stacked** CTA pills = larger thumb targets (Fitts's law) than vodiy's side-by-side pair.

## 5) STEAL LIST (ranked, concrete)

1. **Honest quantitative social proof near the CTA** *(closes our single biggest gap — trust).* Add an honest version of vodiy's stat-trio: a live/growing counter of real questions answered, exams simulated, or readiness-dials completed — plus official-base size and a «база оновлюється / Актуально» freshness badge. Never fabricate a testimonial; keep the integrity positioning. This buys evidence-of-efficacy, the one trust lever we entirely lack.

2. **A whole-page section-template / rhythm engine** *(turns our hero-only craft into full-page craft).* Every section = bold header + muted one-line sub-label + evenly-padded card/tile grid with a consistent icon slot. Carry the hero's production quality below the fold. **Adapt to our palette — keep the single green accent, do NOT copy vodiy's multi-colour icon fills.**

3. **Fix the hero CTA's contrast leak** *(cheap, high-leverage).* Break the green-on-green — give «Почати безкоштовно» a high-contrast fill, add a directional arrow, and add a one-tap Google sign-in beside the no-registration path. Textbook CTA prominence without losing our interactive demo.

4. **Glanceable proof-badge cluster in the hero.** 2-3 concrete stat chips (official ГСЦ МВС base + a hard scope number + a result like 20/20) alongside our differentiated headline — instant scannable credibility our single trust line doesn't deliver.

5. **Below-fold vertical mode-launcher (mobile).** Full-width rows, leading icon + trailing arrow, one task per row — direct task entry.

6. **De-clutter the mobile hero.** Trim the 5-6 line paragraph to one short subhead; drop the wordmark or inline CTA from the top pill so it's just logo + hamburger. Cuts first-viewport cognitive load.

## 6) Honest note on home-team bias

The re-check flagged **two** verdicts for a thumb on the scale — and I'm keeping the corrections, not burying them:

- **Visual craft was corrected from an "ours" win to a TIE.** The original draft inflated vodiy's contained, scannable coloured mode-tiles into a "rainbow catastrophe" (they're a recognised affordance exception, not a failure) *and* under-penalised our below-fold void (both our full-page captures show hero → one section → empty space → footer). Our hero really is more refined; vodiy's proven full-page completeness cancels it. Neutral call = tie. Scoring ours a win there would have been the home-team thumb.

- **Mobile UX bias was noted but did NOT change the outcome.** Two of our four "better-at" bullets were generous ("lower thumb-zone" is a symptom of the dense hero; "calmer single-focus" actually contradicts the density critique — vodiy is the calmer surface). Inflation existed, but vodiy still legitimately edges it; nothing to flip.

**Bottom line on bias:** the honest tally is **3-1-2 to ours**, and it earns the overall nod on message + mechanic. But strip the two most-favourable readings and it's a genuinely close, respectful loss-avoidance rather than a rout. The market leader is beating us where it's hardest to fake — proof and finish. Steal items 1 and 2 are the difference between "nicer hero" and "clearly the better product."
