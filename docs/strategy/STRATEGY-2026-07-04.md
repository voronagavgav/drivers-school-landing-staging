# Drivers School — Definitive Strategy (merged rounds 1–3, 2026-07-04)

## 1. TL;DR positioning

The moat is NOT "honesty" — Vodiy already runs honest, no-false-promise copy verbatim on its landing («Було б нечесно обіцяти 100% результат», https://vodiy.ua/) — the moat is the **quantified, calibrated, decaying P(pass) dial + a named FSRS engine**, which no competitor, UA or international, exposes as a feature (round-3 ua-web + intl audits, zero counterexamples). Lead with the dial as a hard product mechanic backed by confident numbers (the Zutobi/UK pattern: winners convert on quantified proof, not humility), with the 734 restyled images as the 2-second visual hook and calm liquid-glass as texture — an aesthetic verified uncontested across the entire category. Marketing spine: «Лише 1 з 5 складає теорію з першої спроби» (22.6%, multi-period-verified) + «кожна спроба — 250 грн і 10 днів»; monetize the intelligence layer at a ~299–499 грн one-time unlock (market norm confirmed: Vodiy 380 грн one-time, no recurring).

## 2. The moat, evidence-ranked

1. **Calibrated P(pass) dial — CONFIRMED unique, now across all fronts.** Round 1 confirmed no UA *app* has it; round 3 closed the web hole: no audited UA site has anything beyond crude score-threshold heuristics — GreenWay's "≥8.0 average ≈ 100% pass" rule of thumb (https://green-way.com.ua/uk/test-pdd) and the pdrtest bot's "19–20/20 consistently → go confidently" (https://pdrtest.online/). Neither is calibrated, neither decays. Internationally the closest is the UK app's "Smart AI Mode" framed as efficiency, not readiness (https://drivingtestsuccess.com/driving-theory-test-4-in-1-app/).
2. **FSRS spaced repetition — CONFIRMED absent in UA.** Closest is proportional question weighting (GreenWay), which is not memory-model scheduling (https://green-way.com.ua/uk/test-pdd). Germany's Fahren Lernen Max validates the axis with implicit no-wasted-reps copy («von leicht nach schwer ohne Dopplungen», https://www.fahren-lernen.de) — so name the mechanic; nobody else does.
3. **Restyled 734-image house style — CONFIRMED (rounds 1+3).** Competitors reuse official illustrations verbatim (Vodiy/pdrtest/infotech); GreenWay's "realistic 3D" is partial coverage; the pdrtest bot admits ~50% of questions have images at all. Uncopyable-at-a-glance, and it upgrades every SEO question page.
4. **Calm liquid-glass premium design — UPGRADED from opinion to evidence.** Round-3 intl teardown: all winners are bright/gamified/blue-white/app-mockup-heavy; none use a calm premium aesthetic. Genuinely uncontested texture — but texture, not the claim.
5. **"Honesty" as positioning — DOWNGRADED.** Round 1 called honest-readiness the category claim; round 3 shows the incumbent already occupies the honest *copy* («Складіть іспит впевнено, з першої спроби» + explicit refusal to guarantee, https://vodiy.ua/). Round 3 wins: it has verbatim landing-page evidence where round 1 had inference. Keep honesty as brand behavior (dial can go down, no dark patterns), not as the headline.
6. **Forward-looking progress — CONFIRMED whitespace.** All competitor progress UI is backward-looking (attempt history, success %, mistake lists, badges) — infotech, Vodiy, GreenWay alike (https://pdr.infotech.gov.ua/). The state's 900k users + ~80% failure rate is the product argument: access to tests demonstrably isn't readiness.

## 4. Legal verdict (UPGRADED from round-2 opinion to round-3 verified assessment — still not legal advice)

- **Question TEXT: LOW risk** (was "low-to-moderate, opinion"). Law No. 2811-IX Art. 8 excludes acts of state authorities / official administrative documents from copyright (https://zakon.rada.gov.ua/laws/show/2811-20/print), and the ticket bank is formally approved by ORDER of HSC MVS (latest No. 225 of 29.10.2025 — http://web.archive.org/web/20260529001829/https://hsc.gov.ua/index/poslugi/vidacha-posvidchennya-vodiya/pitannya-ta-ispit-z-pdr/), i.e. an official administrative document. Uniform market practice corroborates (vodiy.ua's agreement claims no ownership of questions — https://vodiy.ua/legal/agreement/); no takedowns/lawsuits found.
- **Restyled ILLUSTRATIONS: LOW-to-MODERATE residual risk — our biggest exposure, mitigatable.** Signs/markings are state signs / DSTU 4100, outside copyright (Art. 8 pt 4); but original artistic traffic-scene drawings may be protected, and restyling is pererobka (derivative act). Defenses, in order: (1) illustrations are integral to the order-approved official document (Art. 8); (2) educational free-use, Art. 22(2)(2) permits reproducing visual works as illustrations for education without consent (https://zakon.rada.gov.ua/laws/show/2811-20/print); (3) documented fallback: re-generate scenes from scratch to spec (pipeline already proves the capability).
- **infotech ToS is not a blocker:** it protects their software/compilation, binds only accepting users, cannot privatize official documents (https://pdr.infotech.gov.ua/page/user_agreement). Don't copy their specific rendering or software.
- **Standing constraints:** never claim official status; get a lawyer's sign-off on «на основі офіційних питань ГСЦ МВС» phrasing; **one UA IP-lawyer consult pre-launch specifically on the illustrations** — bounded, one-time, does not block launch prep.
