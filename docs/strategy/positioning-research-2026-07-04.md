## 7. GTM & monetization

- **Invert the freemium gate:** all 2322 questions, explanations, restyled images, exam simulator FREE. Paywall = the dial, FSRS «план до іспиту», calibrated reminders, mistake analytics. Monetize the moat, not the commodity.
- **Price:** one-time/annual ~350–380 UAH, at/under Vodiy's anchor; **web-first checkout** (LiqPay/Fondy) to dodge Apple's 30% and the $9.99–19.99 IAP inflation ([App Store](https://apps.apple.com/ua/app/%D1%82%D0%B5%D1%81%D1%82%D0%B8-%D0%BF%D0%B4%D1%80/id1314961911)).
- **Teaser conversion:** show a blurred/locked readiness number — sell desire, not deprivation.
- **SEO:** every question + restyled image + explanation as an indexable page — competitors are app-store-first; the web layer is a structural gap, and unique images own image search.
- **Content marketing:** TikTok/Reels hard-question hooks over restyled art («90% обирають неправильно»).
- **Fail-safe offer:** «Не склав — доступ безкоштовно до наступної спроби» (outcome-aligned, never a pass guarantee — respects the legal constraint).
- **B2B seed:** автошкола bulk codes — schools care who's actually ready.
- **Cold-start proof:** publish a calibration report ("коли дил показував 80%, складали X%") once data exists; no fake counts.
